Dine In Style

This cozy, elegant restaurant has been open since 2006. Located in Chestnut Hill, this restaurant strives to be at the top of Japanese cuisine by presenting innovative contemporary dishes. With 30 years of experience, executive sushi chef Jim, has dedicated his culinary passion to serving authentic Japanese cuisine with a fresh creative flair. Hokka Hokka is committed to providing guests with the freshest of fish and the highest quality ingredients. Hot dishes from the kitchen and an award winning sushi bar are also other delights of Hokka Hokka.
